Instructions:

  • The aim of this game is to stay alive, and the only way to accomplish that is to kill your opponent.
  • Both of you have a 6-chamber revolver, with 1 bullet in a random chamber already put in.
  • The person who goes first is randomly decided.
  • During your turn, you may pull the trigger on yourself or on your opponent.
  • Your and your opponent’s gun will only kill if it shoots the single bullet in your revolver’s drum.
  • If you shoot at your opponent and there is no bullet, your will consecutively shoot yourself before your turn ends.
  • To shoot yourself, click your character, and to shoot your enemy, click on your enemy.
  • Good Luck!
